# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze E-Commerce sales performance across different states, product categories, payment modes, and customers.

The dashboard helps businesses monitor revenue, profitability, sales trends, and customer purchasing behavior through data-driven visualizations.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Track profit generated from sales.
- Identify top-performing categories.
- Compare state-wise sales.
- Understand customer payment preferences.
- Generate business insights for decision-making.

## Business Insights

### 1. Electronics Generated Highest Revenue
Electronics contributed the highest sales amount among all categories.

### 2. Maharashtra Led Overall Sales
Maharashtra recorded the highest sales revenue compared to other states.

### 3. Cash on Delivery Was Most Preferred
COD was the most frequently used payment mode, contributing the highest transaction value.

### 4. Strong Profitability Opportunities
Several product categories showed strong profit margins, indicating opportunities for business expansion.

### 5. Regional Performance Differences
Sales performance varied significantly across states, helping identify high-performing markets.
